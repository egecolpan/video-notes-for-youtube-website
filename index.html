Features

Everything you need
to learn from videos

Built for students, researchers, and anyone who watches YouTube to learn.

⏱️
Timestamped Notes
Every note is tagged with the exact video time. Click the timestamp to jump right back to that moment.
📸
Screenshots
Capture a frame of the video alongside any note. Screenshots are stored locally and inline with your notes.
🗂️
Dashboard
A full-page view of all your notes across every video — grouped, sorted, and searchable.
📤
Export to TXT or Markdown
Download your notes as a plain text file or Markdown. Timestamps become clickable links in MD export.
🔍
Real-time Search
Filter notes as you type — both inside the panel and across the entire dashboard. Matches are highlighted.
✏️
Inline Editing
Edit any note in place. Press Ctrl+Enter to save or Esc to cancel — no pop-ups.
↩️
Undo Delete
Deleted a note by accident? A 4-second toast lets you undo before the change is committed.
⏸️
Auto-pause
Clicking the note textarea automatically pauses the video. It resumes as soon as you move away.
⌨️
Keyboard Shortcut
Press Alt+N from anywhere on the page to instantly focus the note textarea — no mouse needed.
🌙
Light & Dark Theme
Defaults to your system preference. Toggle manually with a single click — setting persists across sessions.
🔽
Minimizable Panel
Collapse the panel to get out of the way when you don't need it. Your notes are always one click away.
💾
Persistent Storage
Notes are saved to chrome.storage.local keyed by video ID. They survive refreshes and browser restarts.

How it works

Up and running
in 30 seconds

No account. No setup. Just install and go.

1
Install the extension
Add it to Chrome from the Chrome Web Store or load it unpacked in developer mode.
2
Open any YouTube video
The My Notes panel appears automatically in the sidebar on every youtube.com/watch page.
3
Write a note
Type your note and click Add Note — or press Ctrl+Enter. The current timestamp is saved automatically.
4
Jump back anytime
Click any timestamp in the list to seek the video to that exact moment. Your notes persist even after closing the tab.
🔒
Your notes never leave your browser

Video Notes for YouTube has no backend, no account, and no analytics. Every note is stored in Chrome's local extension storage on your own device. Nothing is ever transmitted to any server — not the video content, not your notes, not anything.

You can verify this yourself: open DevTools → Network tab and watch while adding notes. Zero outbound requests.

Installation

Manual install
developer mode

No build step required. Edit files directly and reload in Chrome.

  1. Download the extension
    Clone or download the repository from GitHub.
  2. Open Chrome extensions
    Navigate to chrome://extensions in your browser.
  3. Enable Developer Mode
    Toggle the Developer mode switch in the top-right corner.
  4. Load unpacked
    Click Load unpacked and select the extension folder.
  5. Go to YouTube
    Open any YouTube video — the My Notes panel will appear in the sidebar automatically.
