14:02 § How it works

Thirty seconds
from install to first note.

No account, no setup wizard, no cloud sync to wait for.

  1. Step 01 — Install
    Add it to Chrome.
    From the Chrome Web Store. About four clicks, including pinning the icon to your toolbar.
  2. Step 02 — Open
    Visit any video.
    The My Notes panel appears automatically on every youtube.com/watch page.
  3. Step 03 — Write
    Type. Hit save.
    Press Ctrl+Enter. The current timestamp is captured automatically — no need to look at the clock.
  4. Step 04 — Return
    Jump back any time.
    Click any timestamp in your note list and the player seeks straight to it. Your notes persist across refreshes, tabs, and browser restarts.
18:21 § Privacy
On-device

Your notes never leave
your browser.

No backend. No account. No analytics. Every note is stored in Chrome's local extension storage on your own machine. The video stays on YouTube; your notes stay with you. Nothing is ever transmitted to a server we run, because we don't run any.

No account No telemetry No third-party scripts No cloud sync
